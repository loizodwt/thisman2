In the end, is this man real ?

No, this man is not real. Or at least, this story about a portrait drawn by a psychiatrist's patient is entirely false, invented by Andrea Natella for marketing purposes. Like many urban legends or "creepy-pastas", this hoax worked very well, with so many people believing the story that they went so far as to create theories to explain the phenomenon, making his man tfamous all over the world.

So why are there so many accounts of this man ? Why do so many people claim to have seen him in their dreams ?

Here is a representative diagram of potential explanations for the appearance of this man in dreams.

  • 1. Cultural Influence: External factors such as media, stories, and cultural narratives contribute to the creation of beliefs and perceptions surrounding this man.
  • 2. Suggestibility: Individuals are influenced by the cultural narrative of this man, making them more susceptible to interpreting their dreams in alignment with the legend.
  • 3. Dream Interpretation: People interpret their dreams through the lens of the legend, attributing significance to any imagery that vaguely resembles this man.
  • 4. False Memories: Exposure to the legend may lead to the creation of false memories, causing individuals to genuinely believe they have dreamed of "This Man" when they haven't.
  • 5. Psychological Factors: Internal psychological states such as stress, anxiety, and sleep disorders can influence dream content and frequency, potentially leading to dreams of this man.
  • 6. Collective Unconscious: The concept of a collective unconscious suggests that universal archetypes and symbols, such as this man, may be activated in the dreams of individuals due to shared human experiences and fears.

Although there is no definitive explanation for why people claim to have dreamt of this man, a combination of psychological, cultural and cognitive factors probably contribute to this phenomenon. Studying these factors can shed light on the complexity of human perception, memory and belief.

It is this idea, among others, that makes the this man story so fascinating. How a gigantic number of unrelated people have been touched by this legend, have testified to it, have discussed their dreams, whether or not they were false.
